1. A physical object is the solution given by Azure to migrate huge amount of data
    1. It consists in receiving by azure one of the following objects:
        1. Data Box Disk --> 8 TB capacity
        2. Data Box --> 100 TB capacity
        3. Data Box Heavy --> 1 PB
    2. The second step is to upload our data to those devices and sending back this devices to Azure
    3. The third and last step is to be aware of when azure uploads our data to the console. In addition, it's going to be encrypted and wiped-clean
